Pregnancy-Safe Eating Guide
Safe
Fully cooked fish & shellfish — grilled mahi-mahi, snapper, grouper, shrimp, lobster, scallops, conch fritters, conch chowder. All great.
Avoid
Raw/undercooked — sushi, ceviche, oysters, beef carpaccio, rare tuna. Skip at every restaurant.
Avoid
Unpasteurized cheeses — brie, camembert, blue cheese, queso fresco. Hard cheeses (cheddar, parmesan) always safe.
Limit
High-mercury fish — swordfish, tilefish, king mackerel. One serving occasionally is fine. Mahi, snapper, grouper are low-mercury.
Limit
Cold deli/cured meats — charcuterie, prosciutto, salami. Fine if cooked hot. Skip cold boards.
Water
Mid-80s with high humidity in May. Puja should drink water constantly — dehydration worsens fatigue and Braxton Hicks. Water bottle everywhere.
Mocktail
Every restaurant here handles mocktails well. Best: Kaya (mai tai base is all juice), Louie's (ask bartender), Matt's (ask for something tropical).
Caffeine
200mg/day limit — about one 12oz coffee. Sunny Eggs and Matt's both have great coffee. One cup, skip the extra shot.
Tip
When ordering: ask how fish is prepared (grilled/pan-seared ideal), confirm cheeses are pasteurized on burrata dishes. Don't stress the grey areas.
